About TempTools

TempTools is a free, fast, and privacy-friendly collection of online utilities built for everyday use.

Our Mission

We believe everyone should have access to simple, powerful tools without creating accounts, paying fees, or compromising their privacy. Whether you need a temporary email for verification, a quick QR code, or to compress an image — we're here to help.

What We Offer

  • Temporary Email — Instant disposable inbox powered by Guerrilla Mail
  • QR Code Generator — Create and download custom QR codes instantly
  • Strong Password Generator — Secure random passwords with customizable options
  • Image Compressor — Reduce image size directly in your browser
  • Text Tools — Word counter, case converter, and more

Why Choose TempTools?

Privacy First

Most tools work locally in your browser. We don't store your data.

No Signup Required

Start using any tool instantly — no accounts, no spam.

Fully Responsive

Works great on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Completely Free

Always free. No premium plans or limits.

Built With Love

TempTools was created as a side project to solve everyday problems quickly and privately. All core processing (except the temporary email) happens directly in your browser for maximum speed and privacy.

We plan to keep adding more useful tools based on user feedback.
