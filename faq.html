Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Is TempTools completely free?

Yes! All tools including temporary email, QR code generator, image compressor, and password generator are 100% free with no signup or hidden fees.

How does the temporary email work?

We use Guerrilla Mail's public service. Emails are received in real-time and automatically deleted after some time. The inbox is public, so anyone with the address can see the emails.

Are my images and data private?

Yes. Image compression, QR code generation, and password generation all happen directly in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

Why can't I receive emails sometimes?

Some email providers (especially Gmail) are strict with temporary email domains. Try using a different sender (Outlook, Yahoo, ProtonMail) or generate a new address.

How long do temporary emails last?

The timer shows 10 minutes. When it reaches zero, a new email address is automatically generated. You can also click "New Address" anytime.

Do you store any of my data?

No. We do not store emails, uploaded images, generated passwords, or any personal information. Everything is processed locally or by third-party services.

Can I use these tools for important work?

Our tools are great for quick tasks, testing, and privacy protection, but we recommend using official services for critical or sensitive work.

Will you add more tools?

Yes! We plan to add more utilities like URL shortener, unit converter, base64 encoder, etc. Let us know what you want to see next.
