Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal data:

  • Temporary email addresses generated via third-party services (Guerrilla Mail).
  • Usage data (e.g., which tools you use) — but only in anonymous form.
  • No accounts, no personal information, no emails are stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We only use data to:

  • Provide the requested tools
  • Improve the website
  • Show relevant advertisements (if AdSense is enabled)

3. Third-Party Services

• Guerrilla Mail API — for temporary email functionality
• Google AdSense — may use cookies for personalized ads

4. Data Storage & Deletion

All temporary emails auto-delete after a short time. Image compression and other tools run entirely in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers.

5. Cookies

We only use essential cookies for functionality. Google AdSense may set additional cookies.

6. Your Rights

You can contact us anytime if you have concerns about your privacy.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out via the contact section on our site.
