Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

At TempTools, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle any information when you use our free online tools.

1. Information We Collect

  • No accounts or sign-ups: We do not collect emails, passwords, or personal accounts.
  • Temporary Email Service: Emails are handled entirely by Guerrilla Mail (a third-party service). We do not store or have access to your temporary inbox contents.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which tools are used most) through Vercel Analytics to improve the site.
  • Cookies: We use minimal cookies only for basic functionality (no advertising cookies until AdSense is added).

2. Third-Party Services

  • Guerrilla Mail: Our temporary email feature uses their public API. Any emails you receive are processed and stored by them, not by us.
  • QR Code Generation: Processed entirely in your browser.
  • Image Compression: All processing happens locally in your browser. Images are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Google AdSense (future): When we add ads, Google may collect data according to their Privacy Policy.

3. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data. Temporary emails expire automatically after a short time, and all tool processing (QR, compression, password) happens only in your browser.

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • Use the site without providing any personal information
  • Clear your browser data at any time
  • Contact us if you have concerns

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will post the new version with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us via the tools on our site or open an issue on GitHub.

TempTools is a free utility website. We are not affiliated with Guerrilla Mail or any other third-party services mentioned.
