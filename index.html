Frequently Asked Questions

Is TempTools completely free?

Yes! All tools are 100% free with no limits, no premium plans, and no hidden fees.

Do you store my data or files?

No. Image compression, password generation, QR codes, and text tools run entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

How long does the temporary email last?

The inbox remains active for approximately 10 minutes. After that, a new email address is automatically generated.

Are the generated passwords secure?

Yes. Passwords are generated randomly on your device using strong character combinations.

Can I use this for important sign-ups?

Temporary email is great for avoiding spam and one-time verifications, but we recommend using your real email for important accounts.
