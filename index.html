About TempTools

TempTools is a free, fast, and privacy-friendly collection of online utilities built for everyday use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TempTools completely free?

Yes! All tools are 100% free with no limits, no premium plans, and no hidden fees. We believe useful tools should be accessible to everyone.

Do you store any of my data or files?

No. Tools like the Image Compressor, Password Generator, QR Code, and Text Tools run entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

How long does the temporary email last?

The inbox stays active for approximately 10 minutes. After that, a new email address is automatically generated when you click "Generate New Email".

Are the generated passwords secure?

Yes. Passwords are generated randomly on your device using strong character combinations. We never see or store them.

Can I use temporary email for important sign-ups?

It's excellent for avoiding spam and one-time verifications, but we recommend using your real email for important or long-term accounts.

Why is everything processed in the browser?

For maximum speed and privacy. Your files and data never leave your device.

Can I suggest new tools or features?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions and are always open to adding useful new tools.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. We do not collect or store personal data. Most tools run locally in your browser.
