Frequently Asked Questions

Is TempTools really free?

Yes. All tools are completely free with no limits or premium plans.

Do you store my data?

No. Image compression, password generation, QR codes, and text tools run entirely in your browser. We do not store anything.

How long does the temporary email last?

The inbox stays active for about 10 minutes. After that, a new email is generated.

Are the passwords secure?

Yes. Passwords are generated randomly in your browser using strong character sets.

Can I use this for important sign-ups?

Temporary email is good for one-time verifications, but we recommend using your real email for important accounts.
